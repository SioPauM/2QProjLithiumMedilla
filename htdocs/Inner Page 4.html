IV. EYE OF THE STORM

(video made using Canva)

Description:

It is just pure pain. Cramming is almost unavoidable. Many students feel overwhelmed with the huge amount of requirements to finish. It triggers a lot of anxiety especially since perio is right around the corner.

After you have done all the requirements for the quarter there is a very short sweet moment of relief. Though this short moment of relief quickly disappears when you realize that the next quarter is nearing and you will have to go through this cycle all over again. “Masakit” - Pau

Advised Course of Action:

Cram. Prioritize requirements with big percentages in your grade such as projects and alternative assessments. Other than that, just cram.

I also recommend that you always recheck khub since the quarter is almost ending there might be some requirements that you missed and haven’t done. “I am a victim T_T, the trauma is real.” - Pau
