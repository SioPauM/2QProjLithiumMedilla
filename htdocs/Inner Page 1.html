18 - LITHIUM

I. CALM BEFORE THE STORM

At this part of the cycle, it is thankfully very calm. You get a steady and easily manageable amount of requirements.

Usually, at this stage, there is no stress and hecticness. That is the reason why this stage is one of the most enjoyable of the four.

One of the biggest mistakes that can easily happen is that people will push back doing their reqs. This is because people start to think they have lots of time to do reqs. Though it is true they have a lot of time now, that quickly fades away.

I advise that at this stage people do their reqs as quickly as possible because with Pisay there are always surprises.

These surprises are sadly usually heavy reqs that require a lot of your time. Since we now live in a pandemic where we all have different situations at home, it is imperative that we try our best to adjust to that.
